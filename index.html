The100Method

The100Method was born during Pilates teacher training. The name has many meanings, but at its core, a philosophy about staying grounded while going after your goals. In Pilates, the Hundred is a foundational exercise, 100 beats of the arms, breathing in and breathing out, a reminder to move with intention and connect breath to your core. That principle is the foundation of everything The100Method stands for. Breathe in, breathe out. Move through your day knowing that every choice flows from your core values and beliefs. And when things don't go as planned, dig deeper. Understand how your beliefs may need to shift to carry you to the next level. #youareenough Brand Design · Web Development · Wellness Coaching
